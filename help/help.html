常見問題解答

你們的產品品質好嗎？

當然！為了證明這一點，我們為所有產品提供 1 年保固。我們的行李箱採用優質零件製成，經久耐用，因此您可以放心，它們的品質一流！

Of course! To demonstrate this, we offer a 1-year warranty on all products. Our suitcases are made from high-quality components and are built to last, so you can rest assured that their quality is top-notch!

我忘了密碼鎖怎麼辦？

我們行李箱出廠預設數字為0-0-0，如果您忘記預設的數字，請將數字從0-0-0變更為9-9。

Our suitcases come with a factory preset combination of --. If you forget the preset combination, please change the numbers from -- to 9-9.

行李箱有保存期限嗎？

行李箱沒有保存期限。損壞的速度會根據使用頻率和使用環境而有所不同，為了延長FREEDOM行李箱的使用期限，請妥善保管它。

There is no expiration date for the suitcase. The rate of wear and tear may vary depending on usage frequency and environment. To extend the lifespan of your FREEDOM suitcase, please take good care of it.

行李箱是哪裡製造的？

行李箱是在台灣本地工廠製造，且經由國家SGS檢驗及品牌耐用度、安全性測試，台灣品質您可以放心購買。

The suitcase is manufactured in a local factory in Taiwan and has been tested for durability and safety by the national SGS. You can purchase with confidence knowing it meets Taiwanese quality standards.

如果購買的行李箱後有問題該如何處理？

如果在購買7天內發現商品有問題，請與本公司客服聯絡，本公司客服專線：+886-02-2954-6666，客服時間為週一至週五 9:00-12:00 13:30-18:00

If you discover any issues with the product within 7 days of purchase, please contact our customer service. Our customer service hotline is +886-02-2954-6666, and our service hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 1:30 PM - 6:00 PM.
